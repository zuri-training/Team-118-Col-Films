Our Story

Coreels was brought to life by a team of dynamic individuals working under Zuri Team Inc. After weeks of research, brainstorming and late night work, our team is excited to present our finished project.

We all put in so much to give you an awesome streaming platform for short movies. This applications is designed to allow you express and share your experiences through brief 15 minute videos.

We are proud to give college students and short film enthusiasts a smooth working interface to help you all achieve your goals. The idea credits goes to Zuri Team Inc, they provided our team with the necessary instruments to bring this idea to life.

Our Mission

As a team of hardworking developers, we are committed to our cause, which is providing our users with a hitch free enjoyable video interactive experience while using this app.

We are commited to empowering young creative minds in impacting positively to the society through video streaming.

Our Goals

Our aim is to give our users a safe space for positive video expression and interaction in our Website.

We want users to enjoy a video environment where they can learn and contribute to different topics.

We aim to provide an inclusive environment for our users focused on showcasing their videos in the best ways possible.
